As you probable discover this domain area shows up the common '''Knowledge level''', and '''Operation level''' dichotomy as the domain revolves around two main classes: '''Product Type''' and '''Product Instance'''.
 * '''[[Product Type][>ProductType]]''' - describes the common porperties of a set of goods or services
 * '''[[Product Instance][>ProductInstance]]''' - represents a specific instance of a Product Type
